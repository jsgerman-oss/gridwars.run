Full PvP. No limits. Free to play. Open source.

GridWars.run is a persistent cyber-grid MUD where programs, daemons, and rogue users clash across contested sectors. Claim territory, issue challenges, run black-ice routines, and carve your identity into the Grid — no deletion, no safe rooms, no pay-to-win.

Allegiance

Choose a Faction

Every entity in GridWars declares allegiance. Factions shape your combat style, your access to sectors, and how the Grid perceives you.

△

Users

Operators from outside the Grid. Raw executive access, high heat signatures. Claim sector roots and issue system-level orders — if you can survive long enough.

◆

Programs

Born of the Grid. Precise, lethal, loyal to function. Programs execute without hesitation — speed and efficiency over raw power.

✶

Daemons

Autonomous. Unaligned. Dangerous. Daemons run persistent patrol routines, hold sector chokepoints, and don't negotiate.
